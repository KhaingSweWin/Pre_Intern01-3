Khin Thida Biography

            Khin Thida Myint is a teacher at University of Computer Studies, (Loikaw). She is passionate about the power of education and enjoys connecting students to bright futures.

            Khin Thida was born on April 13, 1985, in a small town called Kanyutkwin that is situated in Phyu township, Bago division. There are many fishermen around so you can eat many kinds of cheap fish. It’s very hot in the summer and warm in the winter. Now she live in Loikaw, Kayah State with my family. Khin Thida’s father retired from Ministry of Agriculture, Livestock and Irrigation in 2013 and her mother died when she was forty three in 2001. Khin Thida has two older brothers and one younger sister. She occupy the third position in her sibling.

Hobbies

Study is one of her favorite hobbies. The family she has been raising up could not offer a great amount of wealth but encouraged her becoming ambitious for being Highly Educated. Her hobbies include reading, watching movies, listening music and playing zumba dance. She likes sports like football, badminton. She loves to travel.

Education and Job

Khin Thida obtained Bachelor Degree from UCSM in 2007 . In 2009 she started her first career at Technological University, (Loikaw).In 2014, she transfered to Computer Universities, (Loikaw) as she always had a keen interest in computer science.In 2020 she obtained Master Degree from UCSM. Now she is working in the University of Computer Studies, (Loikaw). She really love being a teacher because she love to learn and her students teach her something new every day.

Belief

Personalities

She is a socially reactive person but sometime proactive. She approaches to people with positive behavior. She loves meeting people and making friends with people who are social. She try her best to be humble, kind and respectful of everybody she meet regardless of their age, gender, family background, nationality or race. Her lives centers on improving humanity and she love where she is at right now.
